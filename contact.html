Frequently Asked Contact Questions

🌼 How can I schedule a personalized garden consultation?

Simply fill out the contact form selecting "Gardening Advice" or give us a call. Our lead horticulturist offers both virtual and on-site consultations for yellow-themed gardens, permaculture setups, and soil regeneration projects. Pricing starts at $75/hour for members.

🌻 Do you accept volunteer applications?

Absolutely! Xterra Green Space welcomes garden stewards, students, and yellow-thumb enthusiasts every season. Email us with "Volunteer Inquiry" in the subject line, and we'll send the application pack. Volunteers enjoy free workshops and seed library access.

📦 Can I order seeds or gardening kits online?

Yes! Our online shop will launch in Spring 2026. Meanwhile, you can contact our team to pre-order heirloom yellow seed collections (sunflowers, golden beets, yellow tomatoes). We accept phone orders and curbside pickup at our MeadowVista center.

🎤 Do you host speaking events or garden tours for groups?

We love hosting schools, gardening clubs, and corporate teams. Reach out at least 3 weeks in advance. Group tours include golden pollinator walk, composting demo, and yellow dye workshop. For pricing, use the contact form with "Group Event".

📞 What if I need urgent gardening help?

Our helpline +1 (800) 589-2378 is open Mon-Fri 9am-5pm PST. For after-hours, drop a voicemail or send an email and we’ll get back to you next business day with emergency care tips.

International inquiries: We collaborate with global gardeners! Please include your time zone and country in your message. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
