Welcome to the Xterra Green Space Cookies Policy. Like a well-tended greenhouse, our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to ensure functionality, analyze visitor trends, and remember your preferences. This policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, and how you can manage them. By continuing to browse our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described below, unless you have set your browser to reject them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device, remember your actions and preferences (like language or region), and improve your browsing experience. Think of them as little packets of sunflower seeds that help us grow a better experience for you. We also use similar technologies such as local storage and pixel tags.

How we use cookies

At Xterra Green Space, we use cookies for the following purposes, always with respect for your privacy:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function (e.g., navigating pages, accessing secure areas, managing cookie consent). Without these, the site may break.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (most visited pages, bounce rates, garden content popularity). We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous aggregate data.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language, region, or if you have previously closed a pop-up notice about workshops.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: We do NOT sell your data, but we may use limited advertising cookies from social media (e.g., Instagram, Facebook) to show you relevant gardening content if you have previously visited our site. These are set only with your consent via cookie banner.

We never use invasive cookies for purposes beyond our stated garden mission.

Your Consent & Control

When you first visit our website, a cookie banner appears, allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies (analytics & functional preferences). You can change your mind at any time. Here’s how to manage cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can block all cookies, delete existing cookies, or set alerts before cookies are placed. Instructions for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are available in your browser’s help section. However, blocking necessary cookies may impair website functionality.
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can disable Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Our Preference Tool: Use the button below to open our simple cookie preference interface and update your choices at any time.

Note: If you disable certain cookies, you may not be able to access some personalized features like saved workshop recommendations or local gardening tips.

Retention of Cookie Data

Cookie lifespans vary based on their purpose. Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies (like _ga or gardener_zip) remain for the period indicated in the table above. After that, they are automatically removed. You can also manually delete cookies via your browser at any time.

Updates to This Cookies Policy

We may revise this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our operations. Any material changes will be communicated via a notice on our homepage or by email (if we have your address). The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review it periodically.

More About Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers have a “Do Not Track” feature that signals to websites that you do not wish to be tracked. Our website currently does not respond to DNT signals because there is no universally accepted standard for how to interpret them. However, we already limit data collection to only what is described in this policy and provide granular controls as explained above. You can always adjust cookie settings manually.

Third-Party Links & Services

Our website may include links to external gardening resources, seed suppliers, or social media platforms. Those websites have their own cookie and privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. We encourage you to read the policies of any third-party sites you visit.
